Default Offer Settings And Preferences

Below are the default settings and preferences that are applied to each of your new listings as they are active in the MLS. These settings can be changed for each individual listing. If enabled, you will receive notifications when your listings go live to remind you to update your settings per listing, if necessary.

Turn On Offers

To take advantage of the offer tools and module, you first have to enable your offer account by clicking the toggle below. Turning on offers will apply the default settings below to all of your new listings as soon as they are on the market.

Default Notification Settings

Decide which notifications you would like to receive, and how you would like to receive them, for both offers on your listings as well as responses to offers you’ve submitted on other agents’ listings.

Publicize When Offers IN Hand

Indicate whether agents and consumers can see that at least one offer has been formally submitted on the property.

Notify Drafting Agents When offer Received

Send notifications to agents who have begun drafting an offer on your listing once you’ve received a formal offer. This will give the agent a sense of urgency to finish and submit their offer in a timely manner.

Receive Notification When Agent Drafts Offer

Receive a notification when an agent begins drafting an offer on your listing. This is not a guarantee an offer is forthcoming, but will give you insight into offer activity on your listing.

Receive Reminders To Update Preferences On New Listings

Once you have a new listing become active, you’ll receive a reminder to update the seller and offer preferences from the original defaults, if you haven’t already done so.

Commission And Fee Defaults

Indicate your default commissions and fees charged on your transactions, for both listings and buyers. Your listing commission and fees will NOT be published to other members of the community, but are very important as they WILL be used when calculating net proceeds on your listing offers. Commissions offered to buyer agents and sub-agents WILL be published on the listing itself and when agents are drafting offers on your listings.
