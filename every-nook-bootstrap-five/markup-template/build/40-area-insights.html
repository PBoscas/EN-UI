Brief summary of Elkridge, Maryland

I have been a Florida resident and worked in real estate here for over 30 years. I specialize in residential real estate sales and new construction homes in Charlotte County and the surrounding area. We are blessed with beautiful weather year round and both tourists and residents who come here enjoy the lifestyle we are famous for. It is a paradise for fishermen, boaters, sun worshippers and shopper s alike. As an expert in the local area, I bring a wealth of knowledge about buying and selling real estate here.

You can count on me for the most current local market information so you can make the best decisions for yourself and your family. Whether you are selling your current home, looking for a vacation home or ready to build your new Florida dream home, I can assist you with all your real estate needs. Call me today. I am never too busy to answer any of your real estate questions.
