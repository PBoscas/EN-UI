30 Years of Real Estate Experience

I have been a Florida resident and worked in real estate here for over 30 years. I specialize in residential real estate sales and new construction homes in Charlotte County and the surrounding area. We are blessed with beautiful weather year round and both tourists and residents who come here enjoy the lifestyle we are famous for.

It is a paradise for fishermen, boaters, sun worshippers and shopper s alike. As an expert in the local area, I bring a wealth of knowledge about buying and selling real estate here.
